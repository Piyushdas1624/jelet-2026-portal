Core Historical Patterns

01
Calculus & Matrices Dominance: Together they represent 75%+ of the Math section. Focus on rank of 3x3 matrices, limits of $1^\infty$ form, Euler's theorem, and first-order linear ODEs.
02
Physics is Numerical & Graphical: 60%+ of questions are numerical. Pay close attention to motion/force curves and thermodynamic phase mixtures.
03
Chemistry is Theory-Heavy: Up to 90% of questions are concept-based (acidic strength trends, Bohr radii ratios, Grignard and alkyne Tollens' tests). It is your primary time-saver.
04
EEE is Direct and High-Yield: Features basic BJT equations ($I_E = I_B + I_C$), node voltage calculations, and digital gate Boolean simplifications.
